ZOLL Foundation Board of Directors

The ZOLL Foundation is a 501 (c) (3)1 nonprofit organization governed by a Board of Directors, seperate and disticnct from ZOLL Medical Corporation. The Board has the responsibility to review applications to identify those that are within the scope and intent of the Foundations mission, and to determine the level of support for such projects.

The Board is comprised of the Chief Executive Officer of ZOLL Medical Corporation and two other representatives of ZOLL Medical who serve at will and two independent directors.

John P. Bergeron

Mr. Bergeron is Vice President and Corporate Treasurer for ZOLL Medical Corporation and has been at ZOLL since August 2000. Previously, Mr. Bergeron was a Vice President at Ionics Inc., a global separations technology company, where he was Corporate Treasurer and Tax Director. Mr. Bergeron served in a variety of tax positions at other multinational corporations. He has a BA in business administration from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst and a master’s degree in taxation from Bentley College.

Ward M. Hamilton

Mr. Hamilton is Senior Vice President for ZOLL. He joined the company in 1992 as Vice President of Marketing. Prior to ZOLL Mr. Hamilton was employed by the City of Pasadena Fire Department, Datascope Corporation and Laerdal Medical. He has worked in the field of resuscitation and devices for more than 40 years. Mr. Hamilton received his BA in Political Science from Hartwick College and holds a master's degree in public administration from the University of Southern California. He serves on the Citizen CPR Foundation Board of Directors and is active in the American Heart Association.

Coreen M. Packer

Mrs. Packer is a former principal of the Garvey Group, Inc a Real estate investment company, and was a Vice President of Bank of New England in the in real estate asset management department. Mrs. Packer received her BA in history from the University of New Hampshire. She is active in many local voluntary community organizations. Mrs. Packer is a Trustee of the Packer Family Charitable Trust.

Richard A. Packer

Mr. Packer who joined ZOLL in 1992. He became President of ZOLL in 1996 and is currently its Chief Executeive Officer. Prior to 1992, Mr. Packer was a Vice President at Whistler Corporation, a consumer electronics company. Mr. Packer holds an MBA from the Harvard Business School, as well as an engineering degree from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. He is a former Board Member of Mass Medic.

Norman A Paradis MD

Dr. Paradis is visiting Professor of Medicine, Geisel School of Medicine Dartmouth College and Clinical Associate Professor of Emergency Medicine, Keck School of Medicine University of Southern California. He is also an Attending Physician at Dartmouth Hitchcock Hospital Lebanon NH and LAC-USC Medical Center, Los Angeles CA. He is Board Certified in Emergency Medicine. Dr. Paradis has extensive executive experience in medical devices and diagnostics has authored more than one hundred scientific papers and articles and is the Coeditor of Cardiac Arrest The Science and Practice of Resuscitation Medicine.
