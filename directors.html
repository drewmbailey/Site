Board of Directors

The Foundation is separate and distinct from ZOLL Medical Corporation, and as a 501 (c) (3) nonprofit organization governed by a Board of Directors. The Board has the responsibility to review applications for support of activities within the scope of the Foundations mission, determine projects that meet the intent of the Foundation’s mission and determine support for such projects.

The Board is comprised of the Chairman of ZOLL Medical Corporation and two other representatives of ZOLL Medical who serve at will and two independent directors.
